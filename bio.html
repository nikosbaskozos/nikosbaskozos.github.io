Nikos Baskozos is from Athens, Greece. He holds a diploma in architecture (U. Patras) and a master’s degree in music creation for new media (NKUA). He was actively involved in the Athenian experimental rock scene for several years. In recent years, his main focus has been computer music, particularly working with large sound collections in Max/MSP. He was a guest researcher at IRCAM-STMS Lab and at RITMO Centre for Interdisciplinary Studies in Rhythm, Time and Motion. His music has been performed at ICMC 2026, Évora_27 – European Capital of Culture, Electroacoustic Music Days 2024.
